Hi! I'm Cheree Nielson

I am a hardcore animal lover, advocate, and loveable smart ass. Never underestimate my stature, my dark sense of humor can throw down with the best of them.
I'm a Utah native with a wan·der·lust heart and a life motto of "not all who wander are lost," I have been known to roam about in Rome, get lost, visit every water fountain, and end up at dinner with the Italian mafia. I love spontaneous road trips and adventures with my favorite partner in crime, my 19 year old daughter.
I have a passion for learning and practice in the philosophy that you can never know too much, hence the reason I went back to school in my 40's to become a Web Developer.
A thought-provoking movie, an intellectual conversation, or an intense book paired with a glass of wine, whiskey, or scotch is my idea of a quintessential day.
